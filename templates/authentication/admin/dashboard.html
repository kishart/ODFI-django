    Welcome to ODFI Internal Portal

    This portal contains the standard operating procedures (SOP) used in Municipality Islamic Centers (MICs) and Barangay Community Centers (BCCs) that operate under the ODFI umbrella. All members of the ODFI workforce are expected to comply with all the procedures stated here.

    Sections (1) and (2) in the ODFI SOP comprises (1) ODFI Background Information, and (2) ODFI Vision, Mission, Objectives & General Principles. In order to be consistent with the SOP, the sections below start with Section 3, followed by Section 4, and so on.
